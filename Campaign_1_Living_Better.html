Design Notes

1
Three story sections before the principles ‚Äî "The Reality," "The Shift," and "The Problem" build context as the visitor scrolls. By the time they reach the principles, they understand what geroscience is, why healthspan matters, and why equity is at risk.
2
"The Problem" dark section ‚Äî The emotional pivot point. The dark background creates visual contrast and signals urgency. The question "Who gets to age well?" makes it personal.
3
Principles framed as "AARP's Answer" ‚Äî The section header positions the principles as a direct response to the challenge. The subtitle reinforces that this is practical and equitable ‚Äî not abstract.
4
Principle cards with images and "Why this matters" ‚Äî Each card leads with an image placeholder, followed by the human reason, then the formal language. All six are always visible in a 2-column grid ‚Äî no hidden content. "Share" buttons appear on hover.
